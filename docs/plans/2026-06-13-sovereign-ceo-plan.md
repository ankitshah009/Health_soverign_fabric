# CEO Plan: Sovereign — voice-first AI patient advocate

Generated by /plan-ceo-review on 2026-06-13

Context: Autonomous Healthcare Hackathon (Legion Health x Atlas AI). Demos 6pm.

## Premise (sharpened)
Bill/denial defense is on-theme but commoditized. The wedge is NOT bill-reading
(table stakes). It is **provable, consent-gated autonomy** — the signed Patient
Action Receipt — driven by **voice**. Lead with the moat, use the bill as the vehicle.
A tight one-moment demo followed by a big platform vision is the winning pitch shape.

## Vision

### 10x check
A standing advocate that watches your health-money: auto-ingests every bill/EOB/denial,
audits the second it lands, then voices YOU ("that $4,200 bill just hit, I found $3,200
in errors, want me to file?"). The patient does nothing until the moment of consent.

### Platonic ideal
A portable trust fabric: every action any health AI takes on your behalf flows through
one consent-gated, simulated, Ed25519-signed ledger you own and carry to any provider,
app, or regulator. Verifiable by anyone, owned by the patient. The patient is sovereign
because the proof lives with them.

## Accepted scope (this build)
- Lock the core voice -> Grok Vision -> findings -> consent -> Ed25519 receipt flow; rehearse with one real ER bill.
- Public scan-to-verify receipt page (QR) off existing /api/verify + /api/verify/key.
- Spanish-language voice toggle on the same realtime session.
- Grok-drafted CMS No Surprises Act complaint as an additional output.
- Staged proactive "new bill arrived" trigger that makes the agent speak first.

## Sequencing + ship line
P0 -> P1 -> P2 -> P3 -> P4. Each must leave a working demo. SHIP LINE: if time is short,
ship through P1. P2-P4 are upside. Never let a later add break P0.

## Deferred to roadmap ("What's next" slide)

- Receipt registry + cross-app portability ("carry your record").
- Multi-agent consent management; provider/regulator-facing verification.

-> consent -> signed receipt -> judge scans QR, sees valid -> close on the 10x trust-fabric vision.
